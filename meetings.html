Meetings

We currently meet Thursdays and Sundays at the Espresso Royale cafe [map] on State St and North University. Please check the calendar for meeting times.

There is a parking lot on Maynard St. for anyone driving to the club. It is free on Sundays, and around $1.00/hour other days. All metered street spots are free after 5:00 P.M. on weekdays and Saturdays, and are free on Sundays.

We hope to see you at the club soon!
