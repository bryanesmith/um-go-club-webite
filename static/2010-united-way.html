What is it?

Join us for food, prizes and six hours of go!

Note that this is not an AGA ranked tournament.

Event details

  • When is it? December 4th from noon until 6pm.
  • Where is it? Room 3909 of the Michigan Union, University of Michigan (Ann Arbor).
  • Who can participate? Absolutely anyone!
  • I have a question! Send an email to gc-cos@umich.edu. =)

Why attend?

  • An afternoon of go
  • Food and drinks included
  • Prizes for winners
  • Drawing for early registrants for books by Jonathan Hop
  • Drawing for unlimited access to Insei League KGS private room, courtesy Alexander Dinerchtein (3p)
  • Support the United Way

How many reasons do you need? Register early for the greatest value!

Registration

At the door there will be two options:

  • Registration: $15
  • Registration + t-shirt: $25 (subject to limited availability)

Registration includes tournament participation and food.

Early registration

However, we encourage you to register early. This helps us make sure we have enough food, beverages and go boards.

But what's in it for you? If you register early, you will be entered into drawing for one of three copies of one of Jonathan Hop's books.

Those who preregistered by the Nov 22 deadline will also get t-shirts at the discounted price of $22. Preregistration is over, but a limited number of t-shirts will be available at the door for $10.

Thanks to Jonathan Hop at Sunday Go Lessons for his generous gift!

Proceeds

All net proceeds will go to the United Way of Washtenaw County, which coordinates and provides essential community-building resources.
